Compression Calculator

The most accurate compression ratio calculator for Android. Check it out on google play right here.

Compression Calculator is a very detailed and accurate compression ratio calculator for Android. It will calculate the static compression ratio of an internal combustion engine using the parameters you provide. It will also calculate the effective compression ratio based on boost and altitude. You also get to see the quench distance, and total displacement of your engine. You will have to know a lot about the parts you are using in your engine, but you will get the most accurate answer. It supports both metric and standard measurements as input and you can easily switch between units of measurement.
